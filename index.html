Research Interests

Hi, I'm Sophia! I'm a third year PhD student working with Professor Jennifer Rexford and Professor Maria Apostolaki at Princeton University. My research primarily lies in the intersection of software-defined networking, programmable data planes, and network security. Currently, I'm investigating how to use programmable data planes to more effectively defend against various network attacks directly at the packet-level.
